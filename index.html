我 | About

我是郭泉，目前于广西民族大学人工智能学院担任副研究员。 我于2017年获四川大学获得博士学位，师从IEEE Fellow章毅教授。 我分别于2013年和2010年在这里获得了理学硕士和工学学士学位。 于2019～2020年在美国密歇根州立大学和杜兰大学进行博士后研究工作，与Parisa Korjamishidi教授合作。 于2020～2024年在四川大学计算机学院人工智能系副研究员。
欢迎查看我的 GitHub页面、 领英页面以及 谷歌学术页面。

I am Quan Guo, Ph.D. I am currently an Associate Professor at Guangxi Minzu University, School of Artificial Intelligence. I received my Ph.D. degree in machine intelligence from Sichuan University in 2017, supervised by IEEE fellow, Professor Zhang Yi. I also received my Master's degree and Bachelor's degree from there in 2013 and 2010 respectively. I was a Postdoc (Research Associate) at Michigan State University and Tulane University during 2019~2020, working with Professor Parisa Korjamishidi. I was an Associate Professor at Sichuan University from 2020 to 2024.
Check out my GitHub profile, LinkedIn profile, and Google Scholar page.
