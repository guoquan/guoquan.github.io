我 | About

我是郭泉，目前任四川大学计算机学院人工智能系副研究员。 此前，于美国密歇根州立大学和杜兰大学进行博士后研究工作。 我于2017年获四川大学获得博士学位，师从章毅教授，IEEE Fellow。 我分别于2013年和2010年在这里获得了理学硕士和工学学士学位。
欢迎查看我的 GitHub页面、 领英页面以及 谷歌学术页面。

I am Quan Guo, Ph.D. I am currently an Associate Professor at Sichuan University, Department of Artificial Intelligence, College of Computer Science. I was a Postdoc (Research Associate) at Michigan State University and Tulane University. I received my Ph.D. degree in machine intelligence from Sichuan University in 2017, supervised by Professor Zhang Yi, IEEE fellow. I also received my Master's degree and Bachelor's degree from there in 2013 and 2010 respectively.
Check out my GitHub profile, LinkedIn profile, and Google Scholar page.
